üìò Facebook Ads Setup

Getting Started

  1. Create a Facebook Business Account
    • Go to business.facebook.com
    • Click "Create Account" and follow the setup process
    • Verify your business information
  2. Set Up Facebook Ads Manager
    • Navigate to facebook.com/adsmanager
    • Link your Facebook page to your business account
    • Add your payment method

Creating Your Facebook Ad

  1. 1Choose Campaign Objective
    • Awareness: Brand awareness, Reach
    • Consideration: Traffic, Engagement, App installs, Video views, Lead generation
    • Conversion: Conversions, Catalog sales, Store traffic
  2. 2Set Up Ad Set
    • Define your target audience (age, location, interests)
    • Set your budget (daily or lifetime)
    • Choose ad placement (Facebook, Instagram, Audience Network)
  3. 3Create Your Ad
    • Use the headline generated by our AI tool
    • Copy the ad text into the primary text field
    • Use the CTA for your call-to-action button
    • Upload the generated image or video
üí° Pro Tip: Use A/B testing by creating multiple ad variations with different headlines or images to see which performs better.

üîç Google Ads Setup

Getting Started

  1. Create Google Ads Account
    • Go to ads.google.com
    • Sign in with your Google account
    • Set up billing information
  2. Understand Ad Types
    • Search Ads: Text ads that appear in search results
    • Display Ads: Visual ads on websites and apps
    • Video Ads: Video content on YouTube and partner sites

Creating Search Ads

  1. 1Choose Campaign Type
    • Select "Search" for text ads
    • Choose your campaign goal (website traffic, sales, leads)
  2. 2Set Up Keywords
    • Research keywords related to your product
    • Use match types: exact, phrase, broad match
    • Set appropriate bids for each keyword
  3. 3Write Your Ads
    • Use our generated headline (30 characters max)
    • Adapt the ad text for description lines (90 characters each)
    • Include your CTA in the final URL or sitelinks

üì∏ Instagram Ads Setup

Getting Started

  1. Convert to Business Account
    • Go to Instagram settings
    • Select "Switch to Professional Account"
    • Choose "Business" and complete setup
  2. Connect to Facebook
    • Link your Instagram account to Facebook Ads Manager
    • This allows you to create ads from both platforms

Creating Instagram Ads

  1. 1Choose Ad Format
    • Feed Posts: Square or landscape images/videos
    • Stories: Vertical 9:16 ratio content
    • Reels: Short-form vertical videos
  2. 2Optimize Your Content
    • Use high-quality, visually appealing images
    • Keep text overlay minimal (Instagram prefers image-focused content)
    • Use our generated headline in the caption
    • Include relevant hashtags (5-10 recommended)
üí° Pro Tip: Instagram is visual-first. Make sure your generated images are high-quality and eye-catching. The AI-generated content works best when adapted for visual storytelling.

üéØ General Best Practices

Optimizing Your AI-Generated Content

  • Test Multiple Variations: Use our "Generate Variations" feature to create A/B tests
  • Customize for Platform: Adapt the content length and style for each platform's requirements
  • Monitor Performance: Track which headlines and content styles perform best
  • Stay Compliant: Ensure your ads follow each platform's advertising policies

Budget and Bidding Tips

  • Start with small budgets and scale successful campaigns
  • Use automatic bidding initially, then optimize based on performance
  • Set realistic expectations for new campaigns (allow 2-7 days for optimization)
  • Monitor cost per click (CPC) and cost per acquisition (CPA)

Measuring Success

  • Awareness Campaigns: Reach, impressions, brand recall
  • Traffic Campaigns: Click-through rate (CTR), cost per click
  • Conversion Campaigns: Conversion rate, return on ad spend (ROAS)
